About Me

If you have asked me what I wanted to do in the future when I was just starting out high school, I probably wouldn't have given a clear answer, like many other teens. But, when I first started that Intro to Computer Science course, I knew that there was something there that ignited a spark in me.

From what started out as a dream of developing video games, it turned into a huge passion and love for software engineering as a whole. It's amazing to see how the technology and solutions we create can help shape the lives of today and tomorrow. I always find myself amazed whenever something new comes over the horizon, and working in this industry has only continued to flame my hopes for the future.
